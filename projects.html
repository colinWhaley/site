Projects

I like approaching big problems head on, working with motivated people on solutions that can improve lives.

MentalED is a set of curriclum documents designed to teach middle schoolers mental health concepts from the ground up, taking a biopsychosocial approach to presenting the content. Colin began devising mentalED when he spoke to a middle school class, who ended up asking him questions about mental health.

EnTECH (a portmanteau of engage technology) was co-founded by Peter Hoang and I in Fall 2015, as a club with the Federation of Students at the University of Waterloo. Primarily, the club's mandate is to help older adults use technology to keep in contact with their families, and for recreation.
