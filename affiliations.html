Affiliations

I've had the pleasure of learining from some of the best researchers in the basic, clinical and applied sciences. Please see below my past and present affiliations. 

Present

HCI Games Group, Lennart E. Nacke, PhD: I am assisting with testing Rina Wehbe's game Above Water, which aims to educate its players about panic disorder and general anxiety disorder. Additionally, my research with Dr. Kelly Grindrod was co-supervised by Dr. Nacke.

Past

Digital Medication Management, Kelly Grindrod, PharmD: I evaluated the usability of four different types of counsumer keybaords with older adults for my first honours thesis project. The resulting publication is still in press, but interestingly most older adults preferred the most basic keyboard presented.

IDEA Lab, Jim Wallace, PhD: MentalED was developed with Dr. Wallace, who encouraged me to speak to teachers to get their opinions on what they would like to see implimented in an adolescent mental health curriculum suite. This aspect of the project is still ongoing. 

Mielke Research Group, John G. Mielke, PhD: I took two classes with and did an eight-month research project with the Mielke Research Group, evaluating possible quantum-mechanical explanations for the mechanism of action of lithium in treating bipolar disorder. While I did some wet-lab work, the bulk of my contribution was in the form of writing a systemartic review evaluating different studies involving lithium's modulation of glycogen synthase kinase-3, and how this can be measured by tau-protein phosphorylation levels.
